Privacy Policy

App: PetroSelect application – An standard advisor for oil and gas industry
Developer: Ebrahim Rabbani / PetroSelect
Effective Date: June 17, 2026

1. Overview

PetroSelect applications are engineering reference tool for oil and gas well services, intervention, production and drilling activities . We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

PetroSelect does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically, the app does not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or location
  • Record or transmit your usage activity to any server
  • Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Access your camera, microphone, contacts, or files outside the app

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Project data you create (well name, pressure, temperature, and service conditions) is stored locally on your device only using an SQLite database. This data never leaves your device and is only used to allow you to save and reload your projects within the app. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

4. In-App Purchases

PetroSelect offers a one-time Pro upgrade through Google Play's billing system. All payment transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive, store, or process your payment card details. For information on how Google handles payment data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. PDF Reports

The Pro version allows you to generate PDF reports from your calculation results. These PDF files are created entirely on your device and saved to your chosen location. We have no access to the content of these reports.

6. Internet Permission

The app requests the INTERNET permission solely to support Google Play's in-app billing verification. No user data is sent over the internet by this app.

7. Children's Privacy

PetroSelect is a professional engineering tool intended for adults. It is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
